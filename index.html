Wong Kar Wai

Tribute Page

World renown auteur director

  • 1958 July 17 born in Shanghai, China
  • 1963 emigrated to Hong Kong
  • 1980 earned graphic design diploma from Hong Kong Polytechnic
  • 1988 directs movie "As Tears Go By"
  • 1990 directs movie "Days of Being Wild"
  • 1994 directs movie "Ashes of Time"
  • 1994 directs movie "Chungking Express"
  • 1995 directs movie "Fallen Angels"
  • 1997 directs movie "Happy Together"
  • 2000 directs movie "In the Mood for Love"
  • 2004 directs "The Hand" in movie anthology "Eros"
  • 2004 directs movie "2046"
  • 2007 directs movie "My Blueberry Nights"
  • 2013 directs movie "The Grandmaster"

-Wong Kar Wai (http://www.brainyquote.com/quotes/quotes/w/wongkarwa628415.html")
